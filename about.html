About FPS Reaction Time Trainer

Welcome to FPS Reaction Time Trainer, your dedicated platform for improving your reaction speed and aiming skills in competitive First-Person Shooter games. Our mission is to provide a free, accessible, and effective tool for gamers of all levels to sharpen their reflexes and gain a competitive edge.

Our Mission

We believe that quick reaction times are crucial for success in fast-paced FPS titles. Our goal is to offer a simple yet powerful training solution that helps players:

  • Accurately measure their reaction time in milliseconds.
  • Track their progress over time with best and average scores.
  • Develop consistent muscle memory for faster responses.

Technology Behind the Trainer

Built with modern web standards, our trainer utilizes HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript. This ensures a lightweight, fast, and secure experience directly in your browser, without the need for downloads, installations, or external servers. All your progress data (best score, average score) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage, ensuring your privacy and a seamless experience without relying on external databases.
